About Us

OMRESA care provides top-quality care and disability services in a warm and personalized manner.
Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for elders and individuals with disabilities through the support of trained professionals who understand their unique challenges.
OMRESA care was founded by two elderly individuals who recognized the type of care needed and the availability of suitable caregivers. Both the owners and staff deeply understand the importance of compassionate care and are passionate about the services offered to the local community.

Seniors Care Center

As we age, the risk of falls, chronic conditions, and feelings of isolation tends to increase. However, aging also brings a strong desire to maintain good health and engage in the activities you've always wanted to pursue.

That’s where we come in. At OMRESA care, we provide comprehensive support to help you and your parents navigate the changes that come with aging.

      Here are some activities you may enjoy at our day centers:
    • Play Chess / Carrom / Table Tennis / Billiard / Antakshari / Karaoke with other seniors
    • Take a walk in the garden
    • Enjoy refreshments and meals provided throughout the day
